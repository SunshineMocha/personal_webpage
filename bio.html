Biography

In depth, the school years.

I was born in Genoa, Italy on the 5th day of September 1993.
Growing up I started to get interested in the IT field, at the age of 7 when I saw one of the school computer machines I was already infatuated by the aura that piece of old tech emanated.
Not only that, but with a great family friend already working around the same area,
I managed to grasp some knowledge from him that eventually led me to chose an IT course as my study address during High School years in the ITIS Majorana-Giorgi.
Although those specialization years didn't go very well for multiple reasons, I still managed to get a 70/100 grade on the final exams.

In depth, recent days and what I'm doing now.

After graduating in 2013, as I could't afford University, I started looking around for a job.
Through June 2014 to January 2015 I worked as a grocery shop employee, starting to build some relationship skills with the customers
and, at the same time, studying for getting attested as a pizza maker.
Unfortunately, after the end of the contract, they decided the help wasn't needed anymore, so I started looking around once again.
Through June 2015 to September 2015 I started working on another grocery shop for a short period of time but, once again, when the contract ended the help wasnt needed anymore.
What came next was probably the longest work experience I ever had so far, working as a fishmonger from October 2015 to June 2017 when I decided to quit because I wasn't satisfied.
After a while, I started helping my father with his work for a while, working in furnitures until a big grocery store chain called me for a job that lasted 11 months (ended Jun 2021)
The past year has been spent looking through job applications and, with the help of the Axia Group I managed to find a course to reintroduce me for good in the IT world.
This website is the result of the very first 2 lessons of the course, I do apologies for the lack of customisation but as today (March, 9th) we're still learning.
